Important Requirements

  • Mandate: All Vacation Rental Brokers must make customers register for a TDT#
  • Payment Requirements: When sending funds to Tax Collector, payments must include TDT#
  • Active Status: Only collect TDT for properties with active TDT numbers
  • Verification: Verify TDT status before each rental period
