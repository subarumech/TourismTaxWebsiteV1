Who Needs to Register?

You must register for a TDT (Tourist Development Tax) number if you:

  • Rent your property for transient accommodations (less than 6 months)
  • List your property on platforms like Airbnb, VRBO, Booking.com, etc.
  • Operate short-term vacation rentals
